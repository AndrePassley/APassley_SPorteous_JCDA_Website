About JCDA

JCDA was formed to bring people together around practical community improvement. The organization believes that lasting change begins when citizens, schools, local businesses and volunteers work together.

Vision, Mission and Values

Vision

A Jamaica where every community is united, resourceful, safe, and empowered. Where people work together to create opportunities, care for their environment, and build a better future for the next generation.

Mission

To strengthen communities across Jamaica by creating practical programmes that support youth, uplift families, improve shared spaces, protect the environment, and encourage citizens to work together for lasting community growth.

Values

Service, respect, teamwork, accountability and care for the community.

Short Timeline

  1. 2022: JCDA began as a small volunteer group focused on community cleanups.
  2. 2023: The group added youth workshops and school support activities.
  3. 2024: JCDA partnered with local groups to assist families with care packages.
  4. 2026: JCDA continues expanding its events, projects and volunteer network.
